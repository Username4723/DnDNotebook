# General

A girl with brown hair and green eyes, Casdy always wears casual clothes, such as shorts and a t-shirt if it's warm out or a cozy hoodie if it's a bit chilly. Though she'll say she doesn't have a favourite color, she often wears blue and green.

# Personality

Casdy's a con artist that likes to get a laugh or two. When bored, she'll often play subtle pranks, such as swapping things around in people's pockets, changing the volume on someone's phone to maximum while they're none the wiser, drawing "pinch me" on clothes people are actively wearing, and similar.

# Abilities

Casdy has no magical abilities. However, she is mildly skilled at pickpocketing and distracting people, along with basic lock-picking. In addition, she can lie extremely well, along with being quiet on her feet.

# Backstory

Casdy's never really had anything to lose. Living in and out of homeless shelters and foster homes for most of her life, with nobody to say no to her, she became extremely rebellious, to the point of petty crime just for the fun of it.
