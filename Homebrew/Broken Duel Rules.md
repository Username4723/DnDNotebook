# Characters

- Characters are level 20, with 10 adventures of experience (9-month adventures, 3-month downtime)
- Characters use the Standard Array (15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8) for scores and the determined average for HP
- Each adventure provides 2 spells and 10,000 GP worth of items and coins. Living expenses do not exist.
- Characters will be allowed to purchase their own equipment at standard market rate
- Characters cannot be given items or similar by NPCs.
- Players must provide a log of all actions taken to build a character

# Reference Materials

- Multiclassing and feats are allowed.
- Only allowed reference materials are the PHB.
- The Wish spell is banned for the "Do whatever you want" usage

# Setting

- Characters do not know each other but have legit roleplay reasons for dueling to the death, and have morals that allow them to kill.
- Setting will be determined later.

# Duel

- Characters enter the duel with full HP, maximum hit die, all spell slots regenerated, and with no status effects.
- Characters will have 30 minutes to apply armor, buffs, etc.
- Characters will feel compelled to fight to the death (Automatic fail to resist)

# Player / Character Knowledge

- Characters can use their turn to locate another character (DC 20 Insight) as long as said character is visible from a birds' eye view
- Players are not allowed to know what the other player's build is until after the duel
- No metagaming
